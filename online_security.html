What is Online Security?

Online security is about protecting yourself from cyber threats like hackers, malware, phishing scams, and identity theft. It ensures that your personal data, online accounts, and digital presence remain safe from cybercriminals.

By following essential security measures, you can greatly reduce your risk of online attacks and stay in control of your digital life.
