📌 Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is NetGuard?

NetGuard is an advocacy created by Grade 12 HUMSS students from section Socrates in ERJHSto promote online safety and responsibilityamong ERODians.

2. How can I participate?

You can participate by joining discussions in the Open Forum, spreading awareness, and following best online safety practices.

3. What topics are discussed in the Open Forum?

The Open Forum is dedicated to discussions about internet responsibility, online security, cyberbullying, and digital literacy.

4. Who can I contact for assistance?

If you have concerns, you can reach out to our advocacy leaders, teachers, or administratorsfor guidance and support.
